TOP LEFT WINDOW:
This window displays information on the structure of the repository.  The
heirarchy displays all the master branch, HEAD and commits made by users.

TOP MIDDLE WINDOW:
This window displays accompanying information about the selected commit in
the TOP LEFT WINDOW, in particular the person who made the commit and their
e-mail.

TOP RIGHT WINDOW:
Displays the corresponding time stemp of the selected commit.

BOTTOM LEFT WINDOW:
Tells information about the commit - the author, committer, parent/child
commit, associated branches and changelog.

BOTTOM RIGHT WINDOW:
Window with comments, usually affected files of the commit.
